WHO IS MR HARRINGTON?

The Teacher Everyone Respects

Mr Harrington is the Head Teacher and one of the most respected members of staff. His calm authority means a single look is often enough to restore order in a classroom.

Although many pupils find him intimidating at first, they soon discover that he is fair, patient and quietly supportive. He never expects perfection, only genuine effort and honesty.

Jake gradually learns that behind Mr Harrington's serious exterior is someone who wants every child to succeed—not just academically, but as a person.
