WHO IS RUBY?

The Strongest Person in the Room

Ruby Mason has the rare ability to make difficult things look easy. Whether she's wrestling, helping a friend or facing a challenge, she approaches everything with calm determination.

She has little patience for bullies and never hesitates to defend someone who needs her help. Although she can be fiercely competitive, her kindness and loyalty matter far more than winning.

Ruby becomes one of the most important people in Jake's life, encouraging him to believe in himself long before he learns to do so alone.
