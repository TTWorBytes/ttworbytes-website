WHO IS JAKE?

The Boy Behind the Disasters

Jake has an extraordinary ability to imagine everything that could possibly go wrong—and then find himself living through something even worse.

He worries about school, embarrassment, Ruby Mason and almost anything involving public attention. Beneath all that panic, however, is a kind and fiercely loyal boy.

Jake's family means everything to him. He would do anything for his twin brother Jack, and when the people he loves need him, he usually discovers more courage than he believed he had.
