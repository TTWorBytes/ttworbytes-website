WHO IS Elizabeth MORROW?

The Heart of the Family

Mrs Morrow believes that family comes before everything else. She isn't loud or dramatic, but her calm confidence helps Jake and Jack feel safe, even when life becomes confusing or frightening.

She has a warm, friendly face, an ever-so-slightly plump build and a beautiful smile that seems capable of making people forget their worries, if only for a moment. Jake often notices how quickly her smile spreads to everyone around her.

Whether she's preparing homemade meals, encouraging her boys to do the right thing or making sure every family holiday becomes a collection of happy memories, Mrs Morrow reminds Jake that love is often shown through the little things people do every day.
