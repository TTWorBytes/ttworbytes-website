WHO IS KATIE?

The Girl Who Says Exactly What She's Thinking

Katie Bennett is one of Ruby Mason's closest friends. Confident and outspoken, she has little patience for people who waste her time or ask what she considers silly questions.

Jake's first meeting with Katie leaves a lasting impression. She looks at him as though he's just crawled out from under a stone, making it perfectly clear that he isn't part of her friendship group.

Although Katie can seem blunt and difficult to approach, her loyalty to the people she cares about is never in doubt.
