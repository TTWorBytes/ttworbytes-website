WHO IS MR PRITCHARD?

The Teacher Who Brings the Past to Life

Mr Pritchard has spent years teaching history and has lost none of his fascination with the people, decisions and events that shaped the world.

He believes the best way to understand history is to look beyond names and dates and ask why people made the choices they did. His lessons encourage pupils to imagine what it might actually have been like to live through the events they're studying.

For Mr Pritchard, the past is never truly finished. Every generation can look back, ask new questions and discover something that everyone else missed.
