THE STORY

One Family Holiday. One Hidden Message. One Unexpected Adventure.

A funny and emotional seaside mystery about family, recovery and making every moment together count.

After everything Jack has been through, the Morrow family finally gets the chance to escape together for a proper seaside holiday.

Jake expects beaches, chips and perhaps a few quiet days. Instead, a mysterious message in a bottle leads the family into a trail of clues stretching across the town.

Soon there are caves to explore, monuments to climb, competitions to enter and a mystery connected to a boy called Roger. For Jake, the holiday becomes much more than a break from ordinary life.

AT THE HEART OF THIS STORY

The Holiday They All Needed

Jack is almost himself again, and Jake is determined to enjoy every moment they have together.

“For the first time in months, Jack looks like Jack again.”

Jack has recovered enough to join the family holiday, although he still tires more easily than he once did. To Jake, simply having his brother beside him makes every ordinary moment feel important.

As the mystery unfolds, the brothers explore the town, follow clues and rediscover the easy closeness they had before illness changed everything.

The adventure may begin with a message in a bottle, but the real treasure is the chance for the family to feel whole again.

WHY THIS STORY MATTERS

More Than a Treasure Hunt

The clues may lead Jake and his family across the town, but the most important discoveries are not hidden inside bottles or buried in boxes.

They are found in the moments the family shares: laughing together, trying something new and watching Jack slowly become himself again.

After months shaped by fear and uncertainty, the holiday reminds Jake that happiness does not always arrive through something enormous.

Sometimes the best days are simply the ones spent beside the people you were frightened of losing.
