WHO IS FAISAL?

The Boy with the Biggest Secret

When Faisal Khan joins Jake's school, he hopes for something most children take for granted—the chance to be treated like an ordinary twelve-year-old.

Quiet, intelligent and respectful, Faisal quickly earns the trust of those around him. Although he carries responsibilities far beyond his years, he never allows them to define who he is.

Jake discovers that behind Faisal's calm confidence is a loyal friend who values honesty, courage and kindness above everything else.
