WHO IS RYAN?

The Class Clown with a Good Heart

Ryan Crowe enjoys making people laugh, especially if that person happens to be Jake Morrow. Whether he's teasing a friend or making a quick joke, Ryan has an answer for almost everything.

Although he loves winding Jake up, Ryan knows where the line is. When someone genuinely needs help, the jokes stop and his loyalty shines through.

Beneath the humour is a kind-hearted friend who reminds everyone that school should be filled with laughter as well as lessons.
