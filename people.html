THE CENTRAL CHARACTER

Jake Morrow

“If worrying was an Olympic sport, Jake would win gold.”

Jake is twelve, easily embarrassed and capable of imagining seventeen disasters before breakfast. Beneath the panic is a fiercely loyal boy who would do anything for the people he loves.

First appearance: This Is My Last Day

Appears in this story

JAKE'S TWIN BROTHER

Jack Morrow

“Jack has a way of making difficult days feel smaller.”

Jack is thoughtful, quietly funny and often the first person to notice when something is troubling Jake. Their relationship lies at the emotional heart of the series.

First appearance: This Is My Last Day

Appears in this story

WRESTLER, FRIEND, FORCE OF NATURE

Ruby Mason

“She looks like she could fold me into a thousand pieces.”

Ruby is confident, capable and never lets fear decide what she can do. She values kindness far more than confidence or popularity—and Jake notices everything about her.

First appearance: This Is My Last Day

Appears in this story

JAKE'S BEST FRIEND

Alex

“If Alex says, ‘I've got an idea,’ Jake starts worrying.”

Alex is funny, optimistic and remarkably skilled at turning ordinary moments into unforgettable trouble. Behind the jokes is a loyal friend who always stands beside Jake.

First appearance: This Is My Last Day
