This Is My Big Mistake

My name is Jake Morrow. I'm twelve years old.

THE STORY

One Good Intention. One Terrible Decision.

A funny, heartfelt story about growing up, first crushes and discovering that even the best intentions can have painful consequences.

Jake only wants to help his twin brother, Jack. Surely that can't go wrong...

But one small decision leads to another until Jake finds himself trapped inside a mistake that becomes bigger every time he tries to fix it.

As friendships are tested and feelings become increasingly complicated, Jake discovers that saying sorry is sometimes the hardest thing a person can do.

AT THE HEART OF THIS STORY

The Girl Who Changed Everything

Holly Carter has no idea how much trouble Jake is about to create.

“Holly only wanted to make a new friend. Jake accidentally made things much worse.”

Holly loves astronomy, photography and wildlife. She's kind, thoughtful and completely unaware that two brothers are quietly trying to impress her.

Jake believes he's helping Jack. Instead, every decision pulls him further away from the truth until the mistake seems impossible to undo.

WHY THIS STORY MATTERS

More Than Just a Mistake

Everyone makes mistakes.

Most are forgotten within minutes. Some stay with us much longer.

Jake discovers that doing the wrong thing for the right reason can still hurt the people you love most.

But he also learns that honesty, forgiveness and courage can sometimes repair even the biggest mistakes.
