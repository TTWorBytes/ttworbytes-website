WHO IS JACK?

The Calm in Every Storm

Jack is thoughtful, quietly funny and usually the first person to notice when something is troubling Jake.

The twins are very different. Jake worries aloud, while Jack tends to think before he speaks. Together, they make each other stronger.

Jack gives Jake confidence, steadiness and perspective. Jake, in return, would do absolutely anything to protect him. Their bond lies at the emotional heart of the series.
