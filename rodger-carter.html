WHO IS RODGER CARTER?

The Man Behind the Treasure Hunt

As a boy, Rodger Carter spent an unforgettable family holiday creating a treasure hunt that celebrated the places, memories and adventures that had meant the most to him. Before leaving the chalet, he hid his final note, hoping that one day another family would discover it.

Many years later, Jake, Jack and their family become the unexpected participants in Rodger's carefully planned trail. Each clue leads them to another location, turning an ordinary holiday into an unforgettable adventure.

Although Rodger never intended to become part of someone else's story, his simple act of kindness proves that one person's imagination can create memories that last for generations.
