WHO IS BEN HUNTER?

The Man Who Turns Survival into an Adventure

Ben Hunter is a television survival expert who has built his reputation tackling wild places, difficult conditions and challenges most sensible people would probably avoid.

Confident, energetic and impossible to ignore, Ben believes survival has less to do with muscles than people imagine. Staying calm, thinking clearly and working together are the skills he values most.

When he takes Jake and his schoolmates into the wilderness, Ben intends to push them beyond their comfort zones and show them what they're capable of. For Jake, who would quite like his comfort zone back, this is not entirely good news.
