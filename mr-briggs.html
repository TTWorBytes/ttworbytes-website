WHO IS MR BRIGGS?

The Coach Who Believes in Everyone

Mr Briggs is the school's P.E. teacher and wrestling coach. His impressive physique and booming voice can make new pupils nervous, but they soon discover he is one of the school's most supportive teachers.

He believes that effort matters far more than natural talent. Every pupil deserves encouragement, whether they're competing for a county title or simply trying something new for the first time.

Mr Briggs has little patience for excuses, but endless patience for pupils who are prepared to work hard, learn from their mistakes and never give up.
